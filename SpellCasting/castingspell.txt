Design about Casting Spell Game:
Use three class to represent "Player", "Spell", and casting spell action ("CastSpell").
The Spell Casting System, "SpellCastingSystem", is the game engine.
Game engine can deal with as many as spells as needed, but only one kind of spell as requirment.
Game engine performs every casting spell actions.
Game engine must be refresh every tick period to update the status.
Only needed methods of above two are exposed to public.
main function is a tester.
